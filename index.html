Continental Fusion Bistro

This application allows you to seamlessly add and remove meals from your cart, mimicking the experience of ordering food online. Once you have added the desired meals, you can review your selections by clicking on 'Your Cart.' Additionally, you have the option to place an order after finalizing your meal choices. Upon clicking 'Complete' and providing your address information, you will receive confirmation on whether your order was successfully placed. The order details are then stored in Firebase.js, and you can view the details of your latest order in the console.

Tech Stack: React, CSS, Firebase.
